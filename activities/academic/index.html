Interesting case presentation

Every month, on the last Thursday, upto 3 PGs or UGs present an interesting case that they come across, or discuss the results of a study they did.

Language exchange program

To benefit students from outside Karnataka, a language exchange program that involves students proficient in Kannada teaching the language to others, and vice versa with Hindi or Malayalam
