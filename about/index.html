College

Mysore Medical College & Research Institute is situated adjacent to the railway station in the heart of Mysore city. It is closely associated with KR Hospital (fondly called dodda aaspathri by people), Cheluvamba Hospital, Jayadeva Hospital, PKTB hospital.
